
Licence Plate 3 - v1 2021-11-22 11:22pm
==============================

This dataset was exported via roboflow.ai on November 23, 2021 at 1:06 AM GMT

It includes 1318 images.
Licenseplate are annotated in YOLO v5 PyTorch format.

The following pre-processing was applied to each image:
* Auto-orientation of pixel data (with EXIF-orientation stripping)
* Resize to 416x416 (Stretch)

No image augmentation techniques were applied.
